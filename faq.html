Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I replace hearing aid batteries?

For non-rechargeable hearing aids, batteries typically last 3-7 days depending on usage and battery size. Factors like streaming, volume level, and hearing aid type affect battery life. Rechargeable models simply charge overnight and provide all-day power, eliminating battery replacement hassle.

Our Story

Prithvi Hearing Systems operates a specialized hearing aid service facility in Kerala.

We have a service laboratory and otoplastic lab, specializing in customizing hearing instruments and related accessories.

These services are overseen by a team with over two decades of experience in the field. Led by Sadheep Sivanandan, we provide personalized hearing solutions based on our experience and technical expertise.

The Challenge We Solve

There are hundreds of Audiology and hearing aid centers all over Kerala who all dispense various brands of hearing aids which are, mostly European and west-based companies, and the distributors are all based in metro cities like Delhi, Mumbai and Bengaluru.

In the business there is a vital necessity of technical after-sale support, and this has to be given by the company which is not there in Kerala.

The centers sent the hearing aid collected from customers for service to the above cities. It takes minimum 8–10 days to get serviced and send back.

Our Commitment

Prithvi Hearing Systems is dedicated to improving the customer experience and reducing service times for hearing aid users in Kerala.

Our commitment is to bring down the service period from the current 8–10 days to just 2 days in 40% of cases.

By doing so, we aim to provide faster, more efficient technical support, ensuring that our customers can enjoy uninterrupted and high-quality hearing assistance, ultimately enhancing their overall quality of life.
